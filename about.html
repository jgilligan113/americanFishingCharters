About American Charter Fishing

American Fishing Charters takes pride in hard work and low prices. Let us help you create memories that will last a lifetime. If there is anything we can do to make your experience more pleasurable please do not hesitate to let us know. Bottled water will be provided but all other drinks and food must be brought along. A cooler is provided to store all food and drinks (no glass please). There are also four rainsuits aboard in case of that unexpected shower that ocassionaly pops up. All name brand gear that you know and love so you won't lose that trophy fish.
